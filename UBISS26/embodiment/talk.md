## Embodiment

\centering

*Embodiment* is a state of having a physical body.

\vspace{2em}

An *embodied agent* has meaningful sensory-motor interactions with the environment.

## Embodied Intelligence

> - General field encompassing human embodied experiences, study of other living organisms, robotics, dance, etc.
> - EI conference: [https://embodied-intelligence.org/](https://embodied-intelligence.org/)
> - Understanding embodied intelligence helps us shift information processing from the "brain" to the body
> - Reduces explicit computational requirements by offloading
